Privacy Policy - ReelBoost AI

Last updated: April 2026

This policy describes how ReelBoost AI ("we", "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application and related services. This is a template: publish it at the public URL configured in the app (LEGAL_PRIVACY_URL / AppLegalUrls.privacyPolicy) and have it reviewed by qualified legal counsel before relying on it in production or app stores.

1. Who we are

The app is operated by the entity named in your Google Play / App Store listing. Contact: use the in-app feedback email or support channel shown in the store listing.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: email address, password (stored securely on our servers; we do not store plaintext passwords), and optional profile fields (e.g. name, bio, social links, niche) you choose to provide.
  • Content you submit: text ideas, captions, hashtags, and media you upload for analysis (e.g. images or video-related data as implemented in the app).
  • Usage and limits: server-side data about feature usage (e.g. daily post-analysis limits for free accounts) and related security flags where applicable.
  • Device / local settings: theme, language, optional local analytics opt-in (on-device only when enabled), and notification preferences you set in the app.
  • Advertising (if enabled): our app may use Google AdMob. Ads may use advertising IDs and similar technologies as described in Google’s policies.

We do not intend to sell your personal information.

3. How we use information

  • To provide login, profile, and AI-powered features (captions, hashtags, trends, post analysis).
  • To enforce fair use and security (e.g. rate limits, abuse prevention).
  • To show ads where applicable and measure ad performance per Google’s requirements.
  • To send optional local reminders you configure in the app.

4. Service providers

We may use third parties to run the service, including:

  • Hosting / API (e.g. cloud infrastructure).
  • AI processing (e.g. model providers used by our backend to generate suggestions).
  • Email (e.g. password reset).
  • Payments (future in-app purchases may use platform billing; legacy flows may use other processors where applicable).
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) when ads are enabled.

Their use of data is governed by their respective policies and our agreements with them.

5. Data retention

We retain account and usage data as needed to operate the service and meet legal obligations. You may request account deletion where applicable by contacting support; we will describe any limitations (e.g. legal retention).

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices such as HTTPS for API traffic and secure handling of authentication tokens on devices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the age required in your region). Do not use the service if you are under that age.

8. International users

If you use the app from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be processed in other countries with different privacy laws.

9. Your choices

  • Adjust analytics opt-in and notifications in app settings where available.
  • Use platform settings to limit ad tracking where supported.
  • Contact us to access, correct, or delete personal information subject to applicable law.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will change; continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy where allowed by law.

11. Contact

Use the support or feedback channel published in the app store listing or the in-app feedback option.
