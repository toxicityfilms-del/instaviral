## Full description

Grow faster on Instagram Reels with ReelBoost AI — your pocket studio for hooks, captions, hashtags, and content ideas tuned to your niche.

**What you can do**

- **Post analyzer** — Turn an idea (and optional image) into a hook, caption, hashtag set, best-time hint, and audio suggestion.
- **Analyze media** — Upload a photo or video (thumbnail-aware for video) for visual context and a full creative pack.
- **Caption + hooks** — Generate scroll-stopping captions with alternate opening lines.
- **Hashtag generator** — Get organized hashtag buckets to reach the right audience.
- **Reel ideas** — Five fresh ideas for your niche when you’re stuck.
- **Viral score** — Quick feedback on caption length, hashtags, hook strength, and CTAs.
- **Trends** — Browse inspiration lists to spark your next reel.
- **Profile** — Save your niche and bio so AI stays on-brand.

**Free vs Premium**

- Free accounts include **3 AI credits per day** (shared across AI features). Upgrade to **Premium (₹199/month)** for unlimited AI when billing is available.
- Ads may be shown to **free users** only; **Premium** is ad-free.
